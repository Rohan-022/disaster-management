    Social Issues Gallery

    Explore how social issues impact disaster vulnerability and recovery

    Poverty

    Cause

    Unequal resource distribution, lack of education & economic opportunities

    Impact

    Heightened vulnerability to disasters due to unsafe housing, limited resources for evacuation

    Prevention

    Social safety nets, inclusive economic growth, community empowerment

    Pollution

    Cause

    Industrial emissions, vehicular exhaust, improper waste disposal

    Impact

    Respiratory diseases, contaminated water sources, ecosystem damage

    Prevention

    Stringent regulations, clean technology adoption, waste management

    Water Scarcity

    Cause

    Climate change, overuse of groundwater, mismanagement of resources

    Impact

    Crop failures, health crises, migration, conflicts over resources

    Prevention

    Water conservation, rainwater harvesting, sustainable agriculture

    Slum Population

    Cause

    Rapid urbanization, housing shortages, rural-urban migration

    Impact

    Increased risk from fires, floods, diseases; lack of emergency services

    Prevention

    Affordable housing, urban planning, slum upgradation projects

    Overcrowding

    Cause

    Population growth, limited living spaces, economic inequality

    Impact

    Rapid disease spread, difficult evacuations, strain on infrastructure

    Prevention

    Family planning, decentralized development, infrastructure investment

    Waste Mismanagement

    Cause

    Lack of waste processing facilities, consumer culture, poor regulations

    Impact

    Water pollution, disease outbreaks, blocked drainage leading to floods

    Prevention

    Recycling programs, waste-to-energy plants, circular economy models
